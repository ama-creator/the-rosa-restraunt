About Rosa

The Rosa Luncheonette opened its doors on the Upper East Side in 1992. Entering its fourth decade, The Rosa continues to be relevant and cutting edge with the best comfort food in NYC. As food trends change, the The Rosa Chefs invent comfort food recipes that cater to our clientele . Gluten free pancakes, organic and vegan recipes, healthy, stick to your ribs comfort food. The Rosa continues to be the meeting place for Breakfast Lunch and Dinner for New Yorkers, celebrities, and tourists alike.
